## Why Bowl Games Are Different

1. **Neutral Sites** - 40 of 49 games were neutral site. Home field advantage
   signals that drive our regular season edge don't apply.

2. **Player Opt-Outs** - Star players sitting out for NFL draft fundamentally
   changes team quality mid-season. Our team stats become stale.

3. **Motivation Mismatches** - Some teams treat bowls as exhibitions, others
   as championship games. This is unmeasurable.

4. **Long Layoffs** - 3-4 weeks since last game. Momentum, form, and rhythm
   stats lose predictive value.

5. **Unfamiliar Opponents** - Teams that never play each other. No head-to-head
   data, different conference styles.

## Recommendation for Next Season

**DO NOT bet CFB bowl games using the regular season model.**

Options for 2026:
1. Skip bowl games entirely
2. Build a separate bowl-specific model with features like:
   - Opt-out tracking
   - Historical bowl motivation (blue blood vs G5)
   - Days since last game
   - Travel distance to neutral site
3. Reduce bet sizing significantly for bowls
